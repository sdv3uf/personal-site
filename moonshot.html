Growth Intern at Moonshot Insights


A walkthrough of the landing page for the Moonshot Community

From July 2018 to August 2018, I was a Growth Intern for Moonshot Insights, a B2B SaaS startup based in Madison, Wisconsin that predicts the job performance, growth potential, culture fit and longevity of job applicants. I was assigned weekly sprints that I worked on indepedently while following certain guidelines given to me.

One project I worked on was embedding mechanisms in order to measure customer feedback utilizing the software Intercom and Survicate. During this project, I used certain events to trigger a message from a chatbot in order to guage potential customer interest in the software, as well as current customer satisfaction thus far in their trial.

Another project I worked on was an automated lead funnel for dismissed applicants and employers. Utilizing Intercom once again, I created different events in which dismissed candidates and employers would recieve certain emails and messages with information dependent on the situation.

My final project for Moonshot Insights was a paid aquisition campaign in which I created a landing page for a community that customers would gain free access to after signing up for a trial. I utilized mobile.io in order to create this community and used Wix in order to build the landing page. The landing page can be viewed here.
