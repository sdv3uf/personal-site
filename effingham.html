Original Pamphlet

In August 2018, the Radify Labs Innovation Team and I went to Fauquier County, Virginia for a week to do freelance work for a few select wineries. One of the wineries I worked with was Effingham Manor. I was tasked with redesigning their events pamphlet which consisted of ten pages of graphic design I had to rework. On the left is the original pamphlet and on the right is the redesign that I created utilizing the graphic design tool, Canva.
