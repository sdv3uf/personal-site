Freelance Social Media Marketing for Pearmund Cellars

Prototype of a Snapchat filter that could be implemented on the Pearmund Cellars property

Part of the Facebook analytics done using Tableau


One of the other wineries I worked with during my trip with the Radify Labs Innovation Team was Pearmund Cellars in Fauquier County, Virginia.

For Pearmund Cellars, I worked on social media marketing. I created a plan on how to increase their social media presence, which included HootSuite for easy cross-platform posting and a Snapchat filter for their younger customers. I also worked with their social media analytics, more specfically, with their Facebook analytics. Utilizing Tableau, I analyzed their Facebook engagements and determined on which days their posts performed the best and catered the marketing plan to maximize their Facebook engagements.
